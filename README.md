# Tahoe
**Clarity For Your Model Lakes:**
The TUI platform that automates local model testing and training.

### Why Tahoe?

Raw data can be turned into pristine *Data Lakes*. But once that data is used to train, test, and validate your models, the resulting folders quickly becomes a swamp.

**Tahoe is the pristine lake for your models**

It automates away the boilerplate of validatation, metrics, and testing of your models; simply point it to your project. It runs a high-performance, fully bundled engine under the hood to run statistical tests, core metrics, and render crisp visual charts straight to your terminal.
